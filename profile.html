My Story


  • More about me


Hi, I'm Alva Cardona!

Even though it’s been only three weeks since I started taking web design courses at Codetrotters, I’ve always had the drive and desire to learn how to create websites. My work slowly started taking me in that direction… After I graduated college, almost every job I’ve had had to do with the Internet and the web, in one way or another. I was even told by a colleague, early in my career, that I would be working on websites in the future. Little did I know that I would not only be working on websites, but that I would also be creating them.

My knowledge of the web and the Internet eventually grew, more and more by the day. I started taking free online courses and training in Social Media, Graphic Design, SEO, SEM, Blogging and CMS Platforms, AdWords, Ecommerce, Digital Marketing, UI/UX, and eventually, Web Design. It didn’t take much time for me to realize that I needed to learn those skills, as they are as invaluable as they're useful in this century. Almost every business and media outlet use the Internet to reach and engage audiences. Also, all professions related to Information Technology, Information Systems and Computer Science are in high demand, and unfortunately, the job market is lacking in supply. That’s why I became interested in studying web design, because it is a profession in high demand that rewards those who pursue it. I’m becoming more and more curious and interested in web development and programming languages as the course advances, and I will try my best to learn everything my Codetrotters instructors have to teach me.
